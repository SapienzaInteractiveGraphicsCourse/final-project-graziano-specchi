Farm Defender Game
--------------------------
Welcome to the Farm Defender Game!
Theme
It was a nice day, you were taking care of your animals, when...
... a group of hungry wild foxes arrived to your farm, trying to eat the lunch of your lovely friends.
You tried to scare them by screaming and jumping, but it was unsuccessful.
The only thing you can do is to scare foxes by approaching them and threatening them with a stick. Will you be able to scare them
IN 60 SECONDS?
Instructions
W or UP ARROW - move forward
A or LEFT ARROW - move left
S or DOWN ARROW - move backward
D or RIGHT ARROW - move right
SPACE BAR - jump
MOVE MOUSE in order to change direction and look around
LEFT CLICK MOUSE in order to shake the stick
Double ESC to exit
Remember: in order to scare the foxes, you must be close to them, and you must point exactly your gaze toward them!
Don't scare your own animals! You will notice their tails move faster and you will get a lower score!
Don't go too far from the fence! Otherwise your animals will be alone!
You can exit the game, but you cannot pause it! Try your best at once!
-----------------------------------
The more difficult is the modality you choose, the more close you must be to the fox in order to scare it.
